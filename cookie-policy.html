What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website.

They help websites remember information such as preferences, settings, and anonymous usage data.

Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for the website to function correctly.

Examples:

  • Security
  • Form submissions
  • Session management
  • Basic website functionality

These cannot be disabled.

Preference Cookies

Remember settings you've chosen, such as:

  • Cookie preferences
  • Newsletter preferences
  • Form information (where applicable)
Analytics Cookies

Used to understand how visitors use the website. These cookies help us improve navigation, content, and overall performance.

Current analytics provider: Google Analytics

Managing Cookies

You can choose whether to accept analytics cookies when you first visit the website.

You can also change your preferences at any time using the Cookie Preferences option available in the website footer.

Most browsers also allow you to manage or delete cookies through browser settings.

Third-Party Cookies

Some services integrated into our website may place their own cookies, including analytics providers or embedded third-party content.

Those services are governed by their own privacy policies.
